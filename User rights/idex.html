Coffee House User Agreement

By entering and using the facilities of Coffee House Inc., you agree to the following terms:

  1. All customers must purchase a minimum of one item from the menu to remain on the premises. Refills on hot and iced coffee are permitted, but no outside food or drinks are allowed.
  2. Seating is available on a first-come, first-served basis. Customers may not reserve tables or hold seats for others not currently present. Tables are limited to a maximum of 4 persons per table during peak hours.
  3. Disruptive behavior, including loud conversations, use of offensive language, or failure to clean up after oneself, is prohibited. Management reserves the right to ask disruptive customers to leave.
  4. The WiFi network is provided for customer use, but bandwidth may be limited during busy periods. Streaming of large media files is discouraged to ensure adequate connectivity for all.
  5. Coffee House Inc. is not responsible for any lost, stolen, or damaged personal items left unattended on the premises. Valuables should be kept with the customer at all times.

By entering Coffee House Inc., you agree to abide by these terms of use. Management reserves the right to refuse service to anyone who fails to comply.
